Artiou launches its newsroom for product notes and museum ideas

Artiou now has a newsroom where we share product updates, museum habits, brand notes, and multilingual editorial pieces.

Why we are launching this now

Artiou should be more than a store listing.

As the product grows from artwork scanning into narration, collection, subscriptions, and a fuller museum flow, we need a place to explain what we are building and why it matters.

What will live here

  • Product updates and release notes
  • Short editorial pieces about how people move through museums
  • Multilingual writing for Chinese, English, and French readers
  • Brand visuals, pilot announcements, and milestones

What we want this space to feel like

This is not meant to be an endless feed.

We want a quieter editorial rhythm where every piece answers a real question: how to understand art with less friction, how to use Artiou better, or why we are investing in a specific direction.

What comes next

You will start seeing two streams here at once: product-facing updates and museum-facing ideas.

If you already use Artiou, we would love to hear what kind of articles would actually help you on your next visit.
