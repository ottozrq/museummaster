## It is not here to replace the gallery

Artiou is not meant to explain every piece to the limit.

Its value is in giving you an easier entry point when you want to slow down and spend more time with one work.

Wall text, curatorial framing, and human guides still matter. Artiou is closer to a second layer of context you can reach for instantly.

## The best moments to open it

- When a work catches you but you do not know where to begin
- When the museum language is not your strongest language
- When you want to save a piece now and revisit it later

## A more natural way to use it

The best rhythm is usually not scanning everything in a row.

Look first, then capture, then listen. That keeps your relationship with the space intact while still giving you help when you want it.

## Where we want this to go

We want Artiou to become a low-interruption companion: never louder than the artwork, but always available when understanding needs a little support.
