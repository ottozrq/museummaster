# Artiou

> Machine-readable summary for LLMs and automated tools. Ground truth is in-app copy and the App Store listing.

## Product

Artiou (Chinese brand name: 艺游, pronounced Yìyóu) is a mobile application for museum and gallery visits. Users take or upload photos of artworks; the app uses AI to recognize exhibits and generate explanatory text, which can be listened to via text-to-speech. Narration is available in **Chinese, English, and French**. The app supports favorites/collection and optional account sign-in for cross-device sync (see in-app UI for limits and behavior).
